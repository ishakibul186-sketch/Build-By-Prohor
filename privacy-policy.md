# Privacy Policy for Build By Prohor

**Last Updated:** 2024-07-25

This Privacy Policy describes how Build By Prohor ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our website (the "Service").

## 1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect from you, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

### a. Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and set up your profile, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

-   **Full Name**
-   **Email Address**
-   **Phone Number**
-   **Date of Birth**
-   **Address and Country**
-   **Profile Picture**
-   **Biography**

### b. Automatically Collected Information

When you access the Service, we may collect certain information automatically, including:

-   **Device Information:** We collect information about the device you use to access the Service, such as your operating system (e.g., Windows, macOS) and browser type (e.g., Chrome, Firefox). This helps us optimize the application for different platforms.
-   **Geolocation Data:** With your explicit permission, we may collect your approximate location (latitude and longitude) to help pre-fill your country during profile setup. You can enable or disable location services when you use our Service at any time through your device settings.

### c. Project and Support Information

-   **Project Details:** When you submit a project inquiry through our chat system, we collect the details you provide about your business and project requirements.
-   **Support Tickets:** When you contact our support team, we collect the information you provide in your support request.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

-   To provide and maintain our Service.
-   To manage your account and profile.
-   To facilitate communication for project development and support.
-   To process your project inquiries and support tickets.
-   To enhance the security of our platform and prevent fraudulent activity.
-   To comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

-   **With Service Providers:** We use Firebase (a Google service) for backend services like authentication, database, and hosting. Your data is stored on Firebase, and its use is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
-   **For Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 5. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use Firebase's built-in security features, including database security rules, to protect your information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. You can update your profile information at any time through your Profile page. If you wish to delete your account, please contact us through the support page.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our [Support Page](https://build-by-prohor.vercel.app/support)
